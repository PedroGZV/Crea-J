ECR SAMPOS ER-060 S Cash Register Machine

TECHNICAL FEATURES

The SAMPOS ER-060 ECR Cash Register offers advanced performance for a small registerer, thanks to its ease of use and features such as having a thermal printer for tickets and the ability to store an electronic control tape in memory.

  • 28 Direct keys for priced products or Apartments.
  • up to 1000 PLUs (items with description and price).
  • 4 types of VAT.
  • 4 forms of collection (Cash, Check, Credit and Credit Card).
  • 4 currency types.
  • 10-digit alphanumeric display for operator and later for client.
  • 24-column thermal printer (57mm wide paper).
  • Re electronic control box up to 4200 lines.
  • Calculator function.
  • Employee or code training function.
  • Tickets with 6 lines of text and ticket footer with 4 lines of 24 characters each.
  • 8M/4B Small Drawer (340 x 360 x 230 mm).
  • The ECR-SAMPOS ER-060 registerer allows you to work with the ticket disabled and only print the last ticket if requested. Sales and control tape are still stored in memory and save us a piece of paper.
