Cash Drawer 410 RJ11-Automatic

Features

  • Automatic cash drawer. It can also be opened manually with the key.
  • It has an RJ11 connector for automatic opening through the Ticket printer
  • 8 coin compartments
  • 4 metal clip holders.
  • It has a 3-position lock (manual / automatic locking / opening) and keys.
  • Dimensions: Width: 410 mm - Height: 10 mm - Depth: 420 mm
