CASIO SE-C450 Cash Register Machine

TECHNICAL FEATURES

  • Ideal for all kinds of shops: elegant and modern design. Whether for small shops or for cafes, bars and fast food outlets: the SE-C450's modern and elegant design, with its innovative features, fits perfectly into the modern environment of hospitality and commerce.

In addition, using a scanner to register products speeds up the process and prevents possible errors during manual entry.

The optimal combination of the 72-key flat keyboard and side embossed keyboard facilitates the registration process in all retail sectors, especially small restaurants, fast food outlets and grocery stores. Automatic ticket cutting and the paper loading system, which allows you to change the roll quickly and easily, are extremely efficient and time-saving functions in daily work. You can also customize your own tickets with one of the graphic logos from the pre-installed logo base.

The box is easily and seamlessly configured using CASIO C.E.S.(F)-Casio-Easy-Store (Fiskal) proprietary software (optional).
